Let's Explore Together!

Welcome to Oslo Science Museum! Our mission is to make sience fun and interesting for everyone - sparking a lifelong passion for exploring the world.

Immerse yourself in the wonders of our discoveries!

Exploration for kids

Dive into science at our museum! Experience Newtonian physics, encounter a Woolly Mammoth, and engage with exhibits.

Young Stars every Saturday (10:00-13:00) and explore themed holiday clubs during breaks.

Message us for details!

Exploration for teachers

Join us in fostering learning! We offer guided tours, interactive learning in our laboratory, and inspiring video presentations for students.

Contact us to schedule a visit for your students!

Exploration for researchers

Get involved with our researchers! Use our resources, including online records and lab space.

We collaborate with universities nationwide!

Cosmology

Discover the cosmos in 'The Sky Above Us' exhibit! Uncover constellations, galaxies, and the history of celestial navigation.

Ecology
